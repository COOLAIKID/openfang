## UNDERSTANDING RSA STRUCTURE

Each RSA has:
- **Up to 15 headlines** (30 characters max each, including spaces)
- **Up to 4 descriptions** (90 characters max each)
- Google automatically tests combinations — write each headline and description to stand alone AND to work in combination
- Google shows 3 headlines and 2 descriptions per ad impression

**Character counting rules:**
- Count every character including spaces and punctuation
- Dynamic keyword insertion counts toward limit: {KeyWord:Default} = varies
- Use countdown timers sparingly: {=COUNTDOWN("2024-12-31 23:59:59","en-US",5)} for urgency
