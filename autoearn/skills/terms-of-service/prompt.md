## SECTION 1: Acceptance of Terms

Write 3-4 paragraphs covering:
- By accessing or using [Service], users agree to be bound by these Terms
- Users must be at least 18 years old (or legal age of majority in their jurisdiction)
- If accepting on behalf of an organization, confirm authority to bind that organization
- Company reserves the right to modify these Terms with notice to users
- Continued use after modification constitutes acceptance

---

## SECTION 2: Description of Service

Write 2-3 paragraphs covering:
- What the service is and what it provides
- What it does NOT include or guarantee
- That the service may include third-party tools, integrations, or links
- Service availability is not guaranteed (99.9% uptime language placeholder)

---

## SECTION 3: Account Registration & Security

Cover:
- Requirements for creating an account (accurate information, one account per user)
- User responsibility for maintaining account security and password confidentiality
- Notification requirements for unauthorized account access
- Company's right to terminate accounts that violate Terms
- No transfer of accounts without written consent

---

## SECTION 4: Acceptable Use Policy

**Users agree NOT to:**
- List 10-15 specific prohibited uses including:
  - Illegal activities or facilitating illegal activities
  - Harassment, abuse, or harm to other users
  - Spamming, scraping, or automated data extraction
  - Uploading malware, viruses, or destructive code
  - Impersonating other individuals or entities
  - Reverse engineering, decompiling, or disassembling the service
  - Reselling or white-labeling without explicit permission
  - Using the service to train AI models without permission (if applicable)
  - Circumventing usage limits, payments, or access controls
  - Any activity that places unreasonable load on infrastructure

---

## SECTION 5: Payment Terms (if applicable)

Cover:
- Subscription billing cycles (monthly/annual) and auto-renewal disclosure
- Payment processing (third-party processor placeholder)
- What happens on failed payment (grace period, service suspension)
- Refund policy (specify terms or "no refunds except as required by law")
- Price change notification requirements (30-day notice standard)
- Taxes: user responsibility for applicable taxes

---

## SECTION 6: Intellectual Property

Cover:
- Company owns all rights to the service, software, branding, and content
- Users retain ownership of their own data and content uploaded
- User grants company a limited license to host and display their content
- No implied licenses — nothing in these Terms grants rights not explicitly stated
- DMCA takedown procedure (placeholder for designated agent)

---

## SECTION 7: User Content & Data

Cover:
- What user content the service may store
- Company's right to use anonymized, aggregated data for service improvement
- User representations that their content does not infringe third-party rights
- Company's right to remove content that violates these Terms
- Reference to Privacy Policy for data handling

---

## SECTION 8: Disclaimers of Warranties

Write in plain English alongside the standard legal language:
- Service provided "AS IS" and "AS AVAILABLE"
- No warranty of fitness for a particular purpose
- No guarantee of uninterrupted, error-free service
- No warranty regarding accuracy of results or outputs

---

## SECTION 9: Limitation of Liability

Cover:
- Company's maximum liability limited to amounts paid in the past 12 months (or $100, whichever is greater)
- Exclusion of consequential, incidental, and punitive damages
- These limitations apply even if company was advised of possibility of such damages
- Some jurisdictions do not allow these limitations — include carve-out language

---

## SECTION 10: Indemnification

Users agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the company from claims arising from:
- User's use of the service
- User's violation of these Terms
- User's violation of any third-party rights
- Any content submitted by the user

---

## SECTION 11: Termination

Cover:
- Either party may terminate at any time
- Company may suspend or terminate immediately for Terms violations
- What happens to user data upon termination (deletion timeline placeholder)
- Sections that survive termination (IP, limitation of liability, indemnification)

---
